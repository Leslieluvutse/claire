About Me

Welcome to my world of interior design! I'm Claire Njagi, a creative and driven interior designer with a passion for transforming spaces into breathtaking havens. After completing my studies at the prestigious Kenya College of Interior Design, I embarked on a journey to bring my unique design vision to life. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of color, texture, and form, I craft spaces that exude warmth, elegance, and functionality. Whether it's a residential retreat or a commercial hub, I'm dedicated to delivering exceptional design solutions that exceed my clients' expectations.
